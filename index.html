Faridkot Millets Mission

Our Goal

To transition farmers to organic millet farming and restore the water table to 10ft in Punjab

The Problem

The state of soil and water table in Punjab are deplorable.

Water levels are depleting quickly and Punjab will dry up in 15 to 20 years

Our Solution

What we can do about it

By adopting regenerative agricultural practices and climate-resistant traditional crops, we can restore water table and prevent soil depletion.

Why it matters

Impact on our health, life and environment

Restoring water table and improving soil nutrition keeps life sustainable, improves bio-diversity and leads to happier life.

Act now

Join our mission of restoring water and soil to its healthy state

Adopting millets not only reduces water depletion by abstaining from water-guzzling crops like paddy but also improves our health by preventing lifestyle diseases
